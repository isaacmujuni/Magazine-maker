## üé® Design Tips

### Color Selection
- **Blue tones (#4A9FB8):** Trust, professionalism, calm
- **Warm tones (#E67E22):** Energy, creativity, passion
- **Green tones (#52BE80):** Growth, nature, wellness
- **Purple tones (#9B59B6):** Luxury, creativity, wisdom

### Image Guidelines
- **Hero/Feature Images:** 1200x800px minimum, landscape orientation
- **Profile Images:** 800x1000px minimum, portrait orientation
- **Resolution:** 150-300 DPI for print quality
- **Format:** JPG or PNG

### Typography Best Practices
- Keep headlines short (5-7 words max)
- Body text should be scannable
- Use pull quotes to break up long text
- Maintain consistent voice throughout

### Layout Principles
- **Visual Hierarchy:** Most important ‚Üí least important
- **White Space:** Don't overcrowd - let content breathe
- **Balance:** Distribute visual weight evenly
- **Contrast:** Make important elements stand out

## üîÑ Switching Templates

You can switch between templates at any time:
1. Click the template dropdown at the top of the control panel
2. Select your desired template
3. Your current work is auto-saved
4. Edit the new template fields
5. Export when ready

**Note:** Each template maintains its own data, so you can work on multiple designs in one session!

## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Start with the image** - Upload your main image first to inspire your color scheme
2. **Use the color picker** - Sample colors from your uploaded images for cohesive design
3. **Test different templates** - The same content might work better in a different layout
4. **Keep text concise** - Magazine layouts work best with punchy, edited copy
5. **Preview before export** - Check the preview carefully before creating your PDF
6. **Save your work** - Content auto-saves, but keep your uploaded images backed up
